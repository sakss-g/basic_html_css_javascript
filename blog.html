A look into the world

Technology affects almost every aspect of the 21st century, from transport efficiency and safety, to access to food and healthcare, socialization and productivity. It affects the way, individuals communicate, learn and think. It helps society and determines how people interact with each other on daily basis.

With the advancement of technology, the world today has become a global village. Over the years technology has revolutionized the world and our daily lives.

Positive Impacts

  1. Communication around the world has become much more easier with the use of emails, voicemails and social networking sites.
  2. Technology has widened the access to education with large amounts of information and data available online that helps students.
  3. Medical Sector has gained the advantage of technology with the advancement of X-ray, MRI machines and even technological surgical rooms, which is more efficient and essential in saving one's life.
  4. Along with improving business strategies, it has also made marketing easier, more effective and cost-efficient.
  5. Lifestyle has become much easier as most of the items are automated, which makes our life easy, organized, simple and more especially much safer.

Negative Impacts

  1. Technologies such as social media are designed to bring people together, yet higher use of social media tends to make people feel socially isolated leading to depression.
  2. Excessive use of technologies may increase the risk of physical issues such as eyestrain, poor posture and sleep problems
  3. Increase in cyber crime such as hacking, software piracy along with online bullying and blackmailing.
  4. Technology may cause a loss of empathy as people become more accustomed to online violence.
  5. Excessive screen time can cause issues with cognitive learning, language, and social skills in students.
Advancement of technology is a boon to us. It has been transforming lives in various ways. Technology is everywhere and sometimes we even use them without knowing that we are using them.

Technology affects everyone in one or the other way. Similarly, for me technology is something when used properly can do wonders. Taking current situation as an example, due to the ongoing panademic, schools, offices, businesses were closed down but the use of technologies, virtual meeting, distance learning, work from home and many other things were made possible.

During the time of the lockdown, there has been easy access to medicines, groceries, and household essentials with the help of technology like online shopping sites. Adding to this, technology has helped me to be connected with my friends and relatives with the use of social media.

Education feels like a privilege, at this time of pandemic, as I still get to study with the help of online classes. But to the students in remote area where there is still lack of technologies and where physical classes were the only medium for education, really makes me think that the proper use of technology is indeed the way to development and a better future for all.

Apart from this, entertainment is one of the things which has been easily acessible to me. There are many documenteries, movies and so many other things which are available online to keep one entertained.

Getting to use technology has helped me to know the world, while still being at home. It has helped me to access information about the ongoing situation across the world. Having technology at our finger tips, is really an advancement towards developed future.

~ Sakshi Gupta
